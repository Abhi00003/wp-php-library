# Menu Structure for Volatus Aerospace

This document outlines the recommended menu structure for the Volatus Aerospace website.

## Primary Menu Structure

### 1. Home
- URL: `/`
- Description: Main landing page

### 2. About Us
- URL: `/about-us`
- Description: Company information, mission, vision

### 3. Services (Parent)
- URL: `/services`
- Description: Main services page
- **Sub-menu Items:**
  - Drone Inspections (`/services/drone-inspections`)
  - Lidar Services (`/services/lidar-services`)
  - Aviation Services (`/services/aviation`)

### 4. Industries (Parent)
- URL: `/industries`
- Description: Industries we serve
- **Sub-menu Items:**
  - Energy & Utilities (`/industries/energy-utilities`)
  - Oil & Gas (`/industries/oil-gas`)
  - Defense (`/industries/defense`)
  - Forestry & Conservation (`/industries/forestry-conservation`)
  - Government & Public Agencies (`/industries/government`)
  - Infrastructure & Transportation (`/industries/infrastructure`)
  - Construction & Engineering (`/industries/construction`)
  - Mining & Aggregates (`/industries/mining`)
  - Precision Agriculture (`/industries/agriculture`)
  - Property Development & Maintenance (`/industries/property`)
  - Public Safety (`/industries/public-safety`)

### 5. Equipment (Parent)
- URL: `/equipment`
- Description: Equipment and products
- **Sub-menu Items:**
  - Drone Store (`/equipment/drone-store`)
  - Platforms (`/equipment/platforms`)
  - Volatus Engineering (`/equipment/engineering`)

### 6. Training (Parent)
- URL: `/training`
- Description: Training programs
- **Sub-menu Items:**
  - Canada Training Courses (`/training/canada`)
  - UK Training Courses (`/training/uk`)
  - Drones in Ed (`/training/drones-in-ed`)
  - The Drone Mentor (`/training/drone-mentor`)

### 7. Investors
- URL: `/investors`
- Description: Investor relations, financial information

### 8. Contact Us
- URL: `/contact`
- Description: Contact form and information

## Footer Menu Structure

The footer menu should include:
- Quick Links (duplicate of main navigation)
- Services
- Industries
- About Us
- Contact Us
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service

## Setup Instructions

1. Go to WordPress Admin → Appearance → Menus
2. Create a new menu named "Primary Menu"
3. Add all the items listed above
4. Assign the menu to "Primary Menu" location
5. Create a second menu named "Footer Menu" with footer links
6. Assign the menu to "Footer Menu" location

## Notes

- All URLs are placeholders and should be updated with actual page slugs
- Sub-menus will automatically display as dropdown menus
- Mobile menu will show all items in a collapsible format
- Menu items can be reordered by dragging in the WordPress admin
